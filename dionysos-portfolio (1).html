Dionysos Villa

A modern sanctuary on the Athenian hills. Deep charcoal seating, mint‑washed light and brushed brass accents frame views across pine‑covered slopes toward the city basin. Oak, leather, terrazzo and graphite stone compose a tactile rhythm.

Filothei Residence

In the leafy northern suburb of Athens, Filothei Residence embraces Mediterranean ease. Pale limestone, olive‑leaf greens and matte black steel interplay with daylight, shaping calm and elegant interiors for family life and hospitality.
